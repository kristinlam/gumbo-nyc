Games Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass

We're a collective of indie game creators located in Dumbo, Brooklyn. We've formed within a GreenDesk coworking space to inspire and motivate each other, stay current in the indie game makers community, and build out our skill-sharing network. Studios and game lovers are invited to connect or visit us, check out our upcoming showcases and events, and inquire about joining.

Questionable Intent Games is hosting monthly Game Makers co-working days. All game developers, artists, designers, producers, and general enthusiasts in the NYC community who want to get together and work on games together are welcome. Open to makers of tabletop / RPGs, PC / console video games, mobile games, etc.
