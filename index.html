Games Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass

We're a collective of indie game creators located in Dumbo, Brooklyn. We've formed to inspire and motivate each other, stay current in the indie game makers community, and build out our skill-sharing network. Studios and game lovers are invited to connect or visit us, check out our upcoming showcases and events, and inquire about joining.
